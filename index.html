Q&A About IT

What types of vegetables do you sell?

We offer a wide range of fresh vegetables, including leafy greens, root vegetables, organic produce, and seasonal items.

Do you provide home delivery?

Yes, we offer home delivery services for all our vegetables. Delivery charges may vary based on location.

Are your vegetables organic?

We provide both organic and conventionally grown vegetables. Organic products are labeled separately.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by selecting the vegetables, adding them to the cart, and proceeding to checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept online payments through credit/debit cards, mobile banking, and cash on delivery.

Can I return or exchange vegetables?

We ensure the best quality, but if you receive damaged vegetables, you can request a return or exchange within 24 hours.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we provide special discounts on bulk orders for restaurants, caterers, and event organizers.

How do you ensure freshness?

Our vegetables are sourced daily from local farms and stored in temperature-controlled environments to maintain freshness.
